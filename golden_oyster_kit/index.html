Description

Golden Oyster mushrooms are warm-weather mushrooms which grow virorously forming many bright yellow clusters. Similar to other oyster mushrooms, Golden oysters are wood-loving and thrive on oak/elm sawdust and chips. They prefer warmer temperatures of around 75°F ~85°F at a humidity of 95% and take approximatly 1-2 weeks to mature to the size of around 13in.
